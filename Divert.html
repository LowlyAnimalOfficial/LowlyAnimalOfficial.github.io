About Divert

Divert is a software I created to enhance the experience of watching films and series. As an avid collector of great content on my hard drive, I find joy in revisiting them later. The initial version of Divert was purely functional, lacking aesthetic appeal.

Enter Divert V2, an upgraded version that not only functions seamlessly but also boasts an improved visual design. The software efficiently stores data within an SQL database, allowing users to easily track their progress and customize images for each film and series. Additionally, V2 features its own unique logo, giving it a distinct identity.

While the software is still in development, its current version showcases a significant leap in both functionality and aesthetics, offering a glimpse of what's to come in its final form.
