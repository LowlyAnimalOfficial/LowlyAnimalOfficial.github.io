About LavenderBurg

LavenderBurg is a small game where villagers are controlled completely by AI. Their movements are based on the context of the conversation, and you can engage with them on any topic. The next thing in development is world generation. The idea is that it is controlled aswell by an AI. Then after the world is generated, and the villagers spawns, they live their lives in the village, while they know where everything is.

The Game is still in beta and I will continue it when I won't be busy with other projects and school.
